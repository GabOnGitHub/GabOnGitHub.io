My works

Pocket Scammers (PC)

Pocket Scammers is a 3D turn-based RPG developed on OpenGL. The combat uses scam tricks for the scammers to attack you and you can defend yourself by fighting back with skills inspired by actions you can take to avoid falling prey to scams.
I led a team of 4 and developed this game for a project in NYP. I developed the skills system(combat), inventory(to store and equip the skills), shops(to purchase skills), level design and implementation.

PC, OpenGL, C++

Photon Engine Bomberman (PC)

My first project using Photon Engine on Unity. This is a 3D multiplayer bomberman developed on Unity by me. Players can host/join a room and play a game of bomberman against one another. There are also powerups that players can pick up to add modifications such as increasing the range of the explosions from the bombs.

PC, Multiplayer, Unity3D, PhotonEngine, C#

Feed Me! (AR APP)

Feed Me! is a mobile AR game developed on Unity by me with the use of Vuforia. Own a virtual pet slime that you can interact with through your mobile devices. Feed, pat, shower him with love and attention!

Mobile, Augmented Reality(AR), Unity3D, Vuforia, C#

Perrail (PC)

Perrail is a 3D turn-based roguelike RPG developed on Unity. I created Perrail out from heavy inspiration of popular turn-based RPGs like Persona 5, Honkai: Star Rail, Epic Seven.
I enjoyed these games very much and wanted to learn to make my own. I also wanted to further improve my code structure and architect so I decided to add in roguelike elements to learn and also to spice up the gameplay.
The combat of Perrail features skills that have their own multipliers and conditions, an algorithm for the turn order that is proportional to the characters' speed, character animations and tons of other mechanics! This independant project was a real test to my software architect and huge learning curve for me.

PC, Unity3D, C#
